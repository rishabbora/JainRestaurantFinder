The Jain Diet

Jain dietary practices are rooted in the principles of non-violence and are designed to minimize harm to living beings. The following foods should be avoided in the Jain Diet:

  • Meat
  • Seafood
  • Fish
  • Fish Oil
  • Fish Sauce/Oyster Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Potato
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Bamboo
  • Ginger
  • Sprouts
  • Spinach
  • Gelatin
  • Mushrooms
  • Animal Rennet/Enzymes in Cheese
  • Honey
  • Eggplant
  • Tapioca
  • Horseradish
  • Turnips
  • Beets
  • Jackfruit
